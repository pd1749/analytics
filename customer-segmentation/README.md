# Customer Segmentation

Client profiling and segment analysis for targeted marketing.

## Summary

Interactive dashboard identifying customer segments by service preference, spend level, and geography.

## Segments Identified

| Segment | Revenue Share | Profile |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| Premium | 49.9% | High-value, mentoring buyers |
| Starters | 15.2% | Early-career, resume services |
| Quick Service | 11.5% | Immediate needs, lower AOV |

## Findings

- Premium segment has highest lifetime value potential
- Career Starters prime for upsell
- Geographic concentration in NT region
